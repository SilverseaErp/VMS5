New to the VMSV.Group Management System?

If you're new to the VMS, this page will explain how to use it to find the information you need. Click any of the sections below to find out more.

Searching the VMS

Usually, you will know the name of the section of the VMS you need. The quickest way to find it is to use the search feature. On the home page (the page that appears when you open the VMS) this will be found in the centre of the screen. On other pages (including this page), you'll find it on the right near the top.

Enter one or more search words, then either click the icon or press [Enter] on the keyboard. The search will return all pages where the text is found in either the title or its content.

Remember:

  • If you enter more than one search word, only pages which contain ALL the words you enter will be returned.
  • You must enter the full word you are searching for, e.g. if you are searching for "vessel" and search for "vesse" instead, then you won't get any results.
    • The exception to this is plurals and other common word endings. For example, searching for "vessels" will return all pages with the word "vessel" and searching for "ordering" will return all pages containing "order, orders and ordered".
    • If your search doesn't return any results, check your spelling.

Your search results will open in a new page. The VMS will try to order these with the most useful results at the top of the search. Click any blue hyperlink to open the page.

To find some text within any page you have opened, hold down the [CTRL] key on the keyboard, then press the [F] key. Enter the text you are searching for, then press the [Enter] key. Please note that you will need to expand any section you want to search in.

Navigating the VMS

Using the Main Menu

At the top of every page in the VMS, you will see the key sections of the VMS. When you move the mouse cursor over any of these sections, a sub-menu will appear. Hovering the mouse over any of these sub-sections will show if there any sections beneath it (these will have a chevron >> symbol). Click on any item in any menu to open its page.

Using the VMS on smaller Monitors / Mobile Devices

On smaller screens, the navigation menu will not be visible at the top of the screen. Instead, a "hamburger" icon - - will appear at the top-right of the screen. Click this icon to view the menu.

You can expand sections by clicking with the mouse / tapping on the screen. You can show / hide this menu at any point by clicking the icon.

Breadcrumbs

Every page you open will have "breadcrumbs", which tells you where you are in the VMS. You can click any of breadcrumb links (which will be underlined) to move to that parent page.

In the example above, we are currently viewing the Crew Welfare page, which is found under Onboard Crew Management. Clicking Onboard Crew Management will display all the other topics found under that page.

Using the Expanders

Most of the larger pages in this VMS (including this page) use expanders to restrict the amount of information shown on the screen at one time, and to make it easier to find the information you need. Click any expander (these will normally be blue and bolder) to expand / collapse the information beneath. You can also expand / collapse ALL the sections by clicking the icon beside the search bar.

Please note that if you are printing a page from the VMS, you will need to expand every section you want to appear in the printout, before printing the page.

Downloading Forms and Documents

While much of the information in this VMS is found in normal web pages (like this page you are viewing), some information, such as forms you must fill in, are provided in Microsoft Word or PDF format.

The links to download attachments will look the same as links to other pages. However, when you click an attachment link, it will download the file to your computer. If it does not open automatically, click the name of the attachment at the bottom-left of the browser to open it.

Printing

You should be able to access this VMS from any vessel computer, as well as compatible mobile devices. Where possible, you should try to view this information electronically to ensure that it is the latest version.

However, you can still print off any web-page in the VMS. Before you follow the steps below, ensure you have expanded any sections you wish to include in the print-out.

  1. Within the browser (e.g. Internet Explorer or Google Chrome), hold down the [CTRL] button on the keyboard, then press the [P] key.
  2. Select which printer you wish to send the page to (in Chrome, click the [Change] button in the Destination section).
  3. Click the [Print] button.

Revision History

When we make changes to the VMS, they will be applied automatically. If you are viewing a page where changes have been made, you'll notice new text will be highlighted in yellow and older text that is no longer required will be scored out.

Returning to the Home Page

You can return to the home page at any point by clicking the logo at the top-left of the screen

FAQs

The screen doesn't look quite right, e.g. some of the menus are transparent?

This may be because your browser is out-of-date. Speak to the person in charge of IT about updating the browser.

What do I if I spot a problem with the VMS?

For significant problems, you should raise a PCR (Procedural Change Request). This can be found in the form RSQ-08. For minor errors and alterations, please email drisksafe@vships.com.
